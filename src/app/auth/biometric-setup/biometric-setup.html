58.1 · Passkey & Biometric Overview

Password vs. passkey vs. biometric.

Passkeys are cryptographic credentials tied to your device or password manager. Biometrics unlock the passkey locally; Paymo never receives your face, fingerprint, or private key.

Method Security Friction Best Use
Password Medium Typing + memory Legacy backup
Passkey Very high Touch or glance Primary login
Biometric High Instant unlock Mobile app, approvals
Phishing-resistant

Won't work on fake Paymo domains.

Faster login

No typing. Just Face ID, Touch ID, or Windows Hello.

Cross-device

Sync via iCloud, Google, or password managers.

Private by design

Biometrics stay on your device.
