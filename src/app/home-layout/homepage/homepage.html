Section 1.2 · The problem

Running finance across Africa should not require a PhD in fragmentation.

Most operators stitch together dashboards, manual reconciliation, region-specific payout vendors, and multiple KYC tools. Paymo compresses that sprawl into a single operating system.

  • Average African fintech teams integrate multiple providers before reaching stability and settlement consistency.
  • Hidden FX spread leakage compounds when treasury decisions happen across disconnected providers and timelines.
  • Compliance complexity grows when KYC, KYB, sanctions, monitoring, and reporting live across separate tools.

Fragmented stack

  • Six dashboards, separate PSPs, and different settlement views across markets.
  • Manual spreadsheet reconciliation across collections, payouts, cards, and float accounts.
  • Multi-day settlement delays and poor visibility into failed or pending cross-border hops.
  • Hidden FX costs, duplicated KYC work, and weak alerting when routing fails.
