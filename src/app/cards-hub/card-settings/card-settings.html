Card FAQ & Help Center
How do I activate my new card?
In-app: Go to Card Center → select card → tap Activate. Enter last 4 digits and the OTP sent to your phone. Your card is ready to use immediately.
What is the daily spending limit?
Default limit is KES 50,000/day for debit cards. You can adjust this in Card Controls → Transaction tab up to KES 200,000/day.
How do I change my card PIN?
Go to Card Settings → Change PIN. Enter your current PIN, then set a new 4-digit PIN. Changes take effect at the next ATM/POS use.
What is 3D Secure?
3D Secure adds an extra authentication step for online payments. You'll receive an OTP or biometric prompt to confirm each online transaction.
How do I report a fraudulent transaction?
Immediately freeze the card, then file a dispute from Support → Dispute. You can also call our 24/7 fraud hotline at +254 800 999 001.
How do single-use virtual cards work?
A single-use card is created for one transaction. After the first successful charge, the card auto-destroys. Unused balance is refunded to your funding source.
Can I lock a virtual card to one merchant?
Yes. Merchant-locked cards only accept charges from the specified merchant MCC or name. This prevents misuse if card details are compromised.
When is my credit card payment due?
Payment is due on the statement date shown in your billing cycle. You have a 30-day grace period. Minimum payment is KES 2,000 or 5% of balance.
How do I increase my credit limit?
Go to Credit Card → Request Limit Increase. Upload recent income proof. Decisions are typically made within 24 hours.
